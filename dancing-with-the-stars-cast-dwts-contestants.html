    POPULAR reality show Dancing with the Stars has finally concluded on December 5, 2023.

    Here are the celebrity contestants who competed for the coveted Mirrorball Trophy - and the winner who took home with the prize.

    Who competed on Dancing with the Stars this season?

    The season 32 Dancing with the Stars contests were announced on September 13, 2023, on Good Morning America.

    This season's lineup included:

    Ariana Madix

    Ariana Madix is a reality television personality, bartender, actress, model, and author from Melbourne, Florida.

    She is arguably best known for being a cast member on the Bravo series, Vanderpump Rules.

    Charity Lawson

    Charity Lawson is a reality television personality from Georgia.

    She first gained recognition as a contestant on the 27th season of The Bachelor and went on to be the star of the 20th season of The Bachelorette, where she met her fianc√©, Dotun Olubeko.

    Jamie Lynn Spears

    Jamie Lynn Spears is an actress and singer from McComb, Mississippi.

    Aside from being well-known as Britney Spears' baby sister, she is widely recognized for portraying the role of Zoey Brooks on the Nickelodeon sitcom, Zoey 101.

    Jason Mraz

    Jason Mraz is a singer-songwriter and guitarist from Mechanicsville, Virginia.

    He rose to prominence following the release of his debut studio album, Waiting for My Rocket to Come, and has since become known for hit songs like I'm Yours, I Won't Give Up, and Lucky.

    Jason and his partner made it to second place in season 32.

    Mira Sorvino

    Mira Sorvino is a Golden Globe-winning actress from New York City.

    She is known for her roles in the films Mighty Aphrodite, Romy and Michele's High School Reunion, The Replacement Killers, and The Final Cut.

    Xochitl Gomez

    Xochitl Gomez is an actress from Los Angeles, California.

    She rose to prominence on the Disney Channel original show, Raven's Home and The Babby-Sitters Club but her breakout role was as America Chavez in the 2022 Marvel film, Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness.

    Gomez was declared the winner of season 32.

    Adrian Peterson

    Adrian Peterson is a football running back from Palestine, Texas.

    He was drafted into the NFL by the Minnesota Vikings in 2007 and has since had stints with the New Orleans Saints, Arizona Cardinals, Washington Commanders, Detroit Lions, Tennessee Titans, and Seattle Seahawks.

    Lele Pons

    Lele Pons is a YouTuber, actress, and singer from Caracas, Venezuela.

    She is known for her work on the now-defunct social media app Vine, where she was the most followed woman.

    Tyson Beckford

    Tyson Beckford is a model and actor from the Bronx.

    He is best known for his work with Ralph Lauren Polo and was also the host of the Bravo series, Make Me a Supermodel.

    Harry Jowsey

    Harry Jowsey is a YouTuber and reality television star from Australia.

    He first rose to fame in 2020, after competing on the Netflix series, Too Hot to Handle.

    Mauricio Umansky

    Mauricio Umansky is a real estate broker from Mexico.

    In addition to being the founder and CEO of The Agency, which represents the world‚Äôs top celebrities, developers, and resort brands, he is also the subject of the Netflix series, Buying Beverly Hills, and has been seen on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alongside his wife, Kyle Richards.

    Alyson Hannigan

    Alyson Hannigan is an actress from Washington, DC.

    She is arguably best known for her roles as Michelle in the American Pie franchise, Lily Aldrin in How I Met Your Mother, and Willow Rosenberg in Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

    Matt Walsh

    Matt Walsh is a comedian and actor from Chicago, Illinois.

    He is arguably best known for his role as Mike McLintock in Veep, which earned him two Primetime Emmy Award nominations.

    Walsh's participation in the show was announced prior to the start of the season but he opted to pause his participation until the WGA strike comes to an end.

    Barry Williams

    Barry Williams is an actor from Santa Monica, California.

    He is best known for portraying the role of Greg Brady, the eldest of the Brady sons, on the ABC series The Brady Bunch.

    Who are the Dancing with the Stars hosts?

    Since premiering in 2005, DWTS has seen a slew of hosts.

    This season, the show will be hosted by returning host, Alfonso Ribeiro, and new host, Julianne Hough.

    Ribeiro became the host during the show's 32nd season after previously winning the Mirrorball Trophy in 2014.

    Outside of his hosting duties, the actor and comedian is known for his roles as Carlton Banks on The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and Alfonso Spears on Silver Spoons.

    Hough is also no stranger to the DWTS stage, having previously served as a professional dancer, winning twice.

    Ribeiro and Hough will be joined on stage by judges Bruno Tonioli, Carrie Ann Inaba, and Derek Hough.

    Where can I watch Dancing with the Stars?

    In 2022, DWTS shocked fans after announcing that the show would be moved from its usual ABC timeslot to the streaming service, Disney+.

    For one year, the show could only be watched on the streaming platform, however, it was announced before the start of Season 32 that it would be returning to ABC.

    This season, DWTS became the first series to simulcast across both ABC and Disney+.

    All episodes can be streamed on Hulu and Disney+.

    Who is the winner of Dancing with the Stars?

    Xochitl Gomez was announced as the winner of this season's Mirror Ball.

    Fans of DWTS on X, formerly known as Twitter, ripped the show after the winner was announced.
