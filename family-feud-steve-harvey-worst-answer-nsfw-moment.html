    STEVE Harvey has been left staring at the camera after a contestant made the "suckiest" guess the host had ever heard.

    Contestant Dirk's surprising answer to an NSFW prompt was immediately roasted by the beloved game show figure.

    Steve prompted Family Feud contestant Dirk: "Name a person, a man, who you would not want to know uses Viagra."

    "I would say the president of the United States," Dirk confidently guessed as his family clapped.

    The decisive "X" appeared on the screen and the camera cut to Steve who looked beside himself.

    "That was, by far the suckiest answer I've heard since I've been the host," he roasted.

    FAMILY FEUD INDEED

    Steve playfully mentioned his wife during a hilarious segment on the game show recently.

    The TV personality seemed confused when he read off the card: "Name something you think Steve Harvey spends a lot of money on."

    One contestant replied: "[Your] wife, Steve!"

    Steve agreed and shouted: "Yes! That should be the number one answer!"

    Among the correct answers, the board claimed the host spent his wealth on "suits, wife, and women."

    The comedian waved his hands around and even said it should have read as his wife in particular: "Oh no! Not women! Majorie!"

    One fan jokingly wrote on Instagram: "You can hear a small amount of fear in that voice at the end."

    Another added: "Family Feud trying to get Steve in trouble."

    Steve and Marjorie Harvey share their two daughters: Lori and Morgan.
