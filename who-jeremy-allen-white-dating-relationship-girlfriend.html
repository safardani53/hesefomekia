    Jeremy Allen White relationship history: Who has the actor dated?

    THE Bear and Shameless star has had his fair share of high-profile relationships, from casual flings to a loving marriage. Jeremy Allen White's acting career is stronger than ever, but in 2023, it was the Hollywood star's love life that made the most headlines.

    THE Bear and Shameless star has had his fair share of high-profile relationships, from casual flings to a loving marriage.

    Jeremy Allen White's acting career is stronger than ever, but in 2023, it was the Hollywood star's love life that made the most headlines.

    Has Jeremy Allen White been married?

    In late 2013, Jeremy Allen White and actress Addison Timlin sparked romance rumors when Timlin gave an interview to Harper's Bazaar about her love life.

    As reported by People, she said: "I've got a sweetheart who I will not disclose, but, yes, he'd be the guy. "

    "We have a nice foundation from being friends for a long period of time, and then it took a hard right turn into romance."

    Timlin starred alongside White in the 2008 film Afterschool when they were each just 17 years old.

    Since then, Timlin has gone on to act in multiple TV shows and movies, including That Awkward Moment, Californication, Law & Order, and American Horror Stories.

    The duo dated for a few years, and welcomed daughter Ezer in October 2018.

    The couple tied the knot a year later in a courthouse wedding on October 18, 2019.

    Timlin and White kept their romance under wraps for the most part, besides the occasional social media snap.

    On June 29, 2020, Timlin posted a photo of the duo and their daughter on Instagram.

    She captioned the post: "I'm so grateful for my 28th year. I married my best friend of 15 years, who happens to be the love of my life."

    "We have the coolest kid in the world who reveals herself to be a true weird like her parents more and more every day."

    In December 2020, the couple welcomed another daughter, Dolores.

    After winning a Golden Globe Award in January 2023, White gave his wife a shoutout during his speech, saying: "I love you deep in my bones, thank you for all that you do, thank you for everything that you've done."

    Just months later, Timlin filed for divorce in May 2023.

    Since then, the couple have been spotted together a few times, but they've both confirmed they're just co-parenting.

    White and Timlin have agreed to share joint custody of their children, but only under the stipulation that White submits to alcohol testing and monitoring, and attends weekly therapy and AA meetings.

    It is unclear whether Timlin has moved on since calling it quits with White, or if she's focusing exclusively on her children at this time.

    Before his relationship with Timlin, White was briefly linked to his Shameless costar, Emma Greenwell.

    The duo played boyfriend-girlfriend on the Showtime series.

    Besides appearing alongside each other at a few entertainment events, the duo kept their relationship relatively private.

    It's unclear when and why the two broke things off, but White's last appearance on Greenwell's Instagram page was in 2014, shortly after he started dating Timlin.

    Is Jeremy Allen White in a relationship?

    Following his May 2023 divorce, Jeremy Allen White kept a relatively low profile.

    He was likely busy filming the upcoming A24 biographical sports drama, The Iron Claw, based on the life of professional wrestler Kevin Von Erich and the Von Erich family.

    White stars alongside Zac Efron, Lily James, Harris Dickinson, Maura Tierney, and Holt McCallany.

    In July 2023, gossip site Deux Moi received an anonymous tip claiming "a recently separated Golden Globe winner....met this A-List singer-actress during a cover shoot for Vanity Fair."

    Fans speculated that the blind item was about White and Selena Gomez, but neither party responded to the rumors.

    White did get the paparazzi's attention in August 2023, when he was spotted with actress and model Ashley Moore during a Los Angeles outing.

    On August 5, 2023, the duo were photographed kissing, hugging, and enjoying each other's company in a very public display of affection.

    A source later told US Weekly that despite the PDA, the duo weren't “putting any labels on their relationship, but they’re hooking up as often as they can.”

    Moore was previously linked to actor Shia LaBeouf, rapper Lil Twist, and singer Justin Bieber.

    It's unclear how long the two were together, or when their label-less romance fizzled out, as White was spotted with a new woman just a few months later.

    On October 30, 2023, TMZ spotted White and Rosalía spending the day together at a Los Angeles farmer's market.

    White was holding multiple bouquets, while the Spanish singer-songwriter was holding a single, giant flower in her hand, likely a gift from her new beau.

    White and Rosalía were also spotted taking a smoke break together on Wednesday, November 29, 2023.

    TMZ snapped photos of the pair facing each other with their feet touching.

    A source reportedly told US Weekly: “Rosalía and Jeremy Allen White have recently started dating."

    The source added: “They started out as just friends, but things have turned romantic recently.”

    Neither White nor the Chicken Teriyaki singer have commented on or confirmed their rumored romance.

    How can I watch The Bear?

    Jeremy Allen White may be known for his breakout role as Phillip "Lip" Gallagher on the hit Showtime television show, Shameless, but the actor shot to fame in another series, too.

    The Bear premiered on Hulu on June 23, 2022.

    It is produced by FX Productions, an in-house production company owned by FX Networks.

    The show's second season was released one year later, on June 22, 2023.

    The comedy-drama series follows Jeremy Allen White as a young, talented chef who returns to his hometown, Chicago, after his brother's death.

    White stars as Carmen "Carmy" Berzatto, who inherits his late brother's Italian beef sandwich shop, as he struggles with a chaotic kitchen and staff while navigating unresolved personal and family trauma.

    The series features a star-studded ensemble cast, including Ebon Moss-Bachrach, Ayo Edebiri, Lionel Boyce, Liza Colón Zayas, Abby Elliot, and Matty Matheson.

    The show's first and second seasons also feature guest appearances by Jon Bernthal, Jamie Lee Curtis, Joel McHale, Gillian Jacobs, Oliver Platt, and Molly Gordon, among others.

    The Bear received widespread acclaim for it's first season, with critics and fans both praising the show's directing, acting, writing, and production.

    The first season received 13 Emmy nominations, including Outstanding Comedy Series.

    White was nominated for and won multiple awards for his portrayal as Carmy.

    He most notably won the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Comedy Series, the Critics' Choice Award for Best Actor in a Comedy Series, and the Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Television Series – Musical or Comedy.
