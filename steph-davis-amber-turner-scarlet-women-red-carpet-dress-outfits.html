    31 Jan 2024, 00:00

    CELEBS turned out in force for the OK! Magazine’s 25th birthday party with red the colour of the night.

    The star-studded bash saw the likes of Stephanie Davis and Kristina Rihanoff on the red carpet – with dresses to match.

    The mag threw a birthday bash at The Shard, which saw some celebs leave worse for wear after a night toasting the title on March 21.

    Former Atomic Kitten Kerry Katona wowed in a daring red dress with thigh-high split, but was later seen struggling to hold the garment together in the back of a cab.

    Her red dress was on trend with the bold colour being sported by a host of celebs.

    Reality TV star Amber Turner wowed in a red jumpsuit, paired with simple jewellery and matching black bag and shoes.

    The 24-year-old teased her cleavage in the low cut number which emphasised her long legs.

    Fellow TOWIE star Georgia Kousoulou dared to dress in double red in a tuxedo dress with ruffled sleeves teamed with red zip-up boots.

    Tina Stinnes, from fellow reality TV show Love Island, also chose a jacket-style red dress, completed with studded black boots and a matching black belt.

    Celebrity Big Brother’s Steph Davis oozed Hollywood glamour in a floor-length red gown, which she teamed with skyscraper nude heels.

    Former Strictly star and mum-of-one, Kristina Rihanoff, showcased her incredible figure in a lacy red gown, which you could see her underwear through.

    Dancing on Ice presenter Laura Hamilton went for a strapless floor-length jumpsuit, which she paired with low-key jewellery.

    Mum-of-two and former WAG Lizzie Cundy went full-on glam and rocked a floor-length ruffled dress with a key hole cleavage detail.

    And American actress and daughter of The Hoff, Hayley Hasselhoff, turned heads in a red trouser suit, with just one button on the jacket amplifying her assets in a sheer bra.
