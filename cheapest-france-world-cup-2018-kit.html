    WE'RE just weeks away from the start of the World Cup, and France fans will be hopeful that 2018 is their year to add to their trophy collection.

    After the heartbreak of finishing runners-up Euro 2016, France will be backing their talented squad to go deep in the knockout stages of an international tournament once again.

    And if you’re keen to show your support for Les Blues, you might want to buy a France shirt to wear down the pub.

    Of course, football shirts can be pretty pricey if you don't know where to look.

    But luckily, we've searched the web to see where you can buy a France replica shirt for less.

    On the official Nike store, the authentic match day jersey costs £99.95, with the replica pretty pricey at £69.95.

    But the cheapest version we’ve found of France's adult replica shirt is at Sports Direct, which costs £54.99.

    There’s also the option of Kit Bag, which has the shirt at £59.99, or JD Sports, where the shirt is priced at £64.99 – only £5 less than the official store price.

    If you’re in the market for a junior/children’s shirt, they cost £44.95-£51.95 on Nike's online store.

    But we’ve found these cheaper too. We’ve seen the home and away shirts on Sports Direct for £39.99.

    Of course if you really want to stand out this summer, you could look at buying a vintage France kit.

    There’s tons of options from Classic Football Shirts and VintageFootballShirts.com.

    Whether you’re looking for an awesome international shirt from yesteryear or even a favourite club shirt, both sites are well worth checking out.
