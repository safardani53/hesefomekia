    Bold and beautiful Olga Ospina has it all — Ospina has been wowing audiences with her beauty but, more importantly, with her news reports. Fearlessness and a driven approach can be seen in her reporting, as she reports news without hesitation and a will to convey an event thoroughly.

    She has covered a wide range of topics over the years. From natural disasters to breaking news to interviewing world-renowned celebrities, Ospina likes to keep her plate full.

    Apart from her on-screen persona, she is a fun-loving adventurer off camera. She is very fond of traveling, exploring new places, and meeting new faces.

    Keep reading to know more about Ospina’s bio, family, personal life, and career!

    Ospina’s Wiki: Date of Birth Remains Concealed

    The gorgeous anchor Ospina celebrates her birthday on the 27th of June every year; however, her birth year remains undisclosed to date.

    She was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, up to the age of 8, and thereafter, she lived in Colombia with her parents till she became 13. Later, she moved back to the states with her family.

    Her nationality is American as she was born in the USA, but her ethnicity is Colombian.

    Likewise, information regarding her height, high school years, and later education also remains undisclosed as of yet.

    Despite being a well-renowned anchor, Olga has kept most of her personal life away from the limelight.

    On December 16, 2016, Ospina surprised everyone when she shared a picture of herself with her newborn daughter, Sophie, on Facebook.

    But she is yet to disclose anything about her partner or husband (if she is married), with whom she gave birth to her adorable daughter. Maybe, she is just happy with keeping her personal life away from the spotlight.

    Left Fox 11 in 2019

    Since 2012, Ospina was associated with Fox 11 Los Angeles, working as a news reporter and a weathercaster. But she left the network in May 2019 to “embark on a new journey and am starting a new job in media relations.”

    On May 26, 2019, her last at Fox, she made a tweet to thank all her well-wishers and colleagues for supporting her on her long journey.

    “Last day in studio,” Ospina wrote. “I’ve decided to embark on a new journey and am starting a new job in media relations.”

    She added, “I’ve decided to embark on a new journey and am starting a new job in media relations. Excited for what’s to come, but so thankful for the amazing opportunities over the years, for all your support & for allowing me into your homes every week!”
