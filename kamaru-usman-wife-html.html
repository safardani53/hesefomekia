    Kamarudeen Usman, best known as Kamaru Usman, has impressed everyone with his wrestling skills.

    He is a professional mixed martial artist and is currently the reigning UFC Welterweight Champion. He is also number 1 in UFC's men's pound-for-pound ranking.

    With his impressive skills and charismatic personality, Usman has garnered a lot of fans and followers.

    So naturally, most of them are interested in his professional career, but they can't help but wonder about his romantic ventures.

    It is evident from his comment section on his social media that most people want to know about his wife and family.

    Kamaru Usman with his daughter, Samirah, who he shares with his wife, Eleslie Dietzsch. (Source: Instagram)

    Who Is Kamaru Usman's Wife?

    It is reported that Usman is a married man and has a wife. He tied the knot with his long-time girlfriend, Eleslie Dietzsch.

    The UFC Champion rarely posts about his spouse on his social media. Moreover, Dietzsch has kept her Instagram private, so not much information is known about her.

    According to PlayersGF, Dietzsch was born on November 22, 1986. She is of Brazilian descent and was born to her parents, Eunice and Jose.

    It is unknown when Usman and his wife started dating and tied the knot. However, in 2016, the athlete revealed his girlfriend was from Brazil, and he loved the country.

    He said, "I am loving Brazil. My girlfriend is from Brazil, and my daughter is half-Brazilian." Other than this, he has said nothing about his spouse.

    It is evident that the pair have kept their relationship out of the limelight and may do so in the future as well.

    Kamaru Usman's Daughter 

    Usman is a proud father to daughter Samirah. Samirah was born in August 2014, and since then, the UFC champion has been posting about her on his social media.

    As of 2022, Samirah is seven years old, and she is more active on social media than her parents. She has an Instagram by the name @misspretty_14 and has over 15.8K followers.

    Samirah's social media is moderated by Usman and his spouse, who regulate their daughter's interactions online. Dietzsch often appears on her daughter's Instagram, who shares her daily activities with her fans and followers.

    Kamaru Usman's wife, Eleslie Dietzsch, and daughter, Samirah, posing for a photo. (Source: Instagram)

    From celebrating Christmas to going on bike rides with her parents, Samirah gives an insight into her family life. Usman is very close with his daughter.

    He also takes her to his games and events, and they are often photographed together. Samirah also goes on training sessions with her father and posts videos of the two exercising together.

    All in all, Usman is a dotting father who is very active in his daughter's life.

    Even though Dietzsch does not have much social media presence, looking at her daughter's Instagram, it seems like she cares a lot about her family and is always there to support them.
