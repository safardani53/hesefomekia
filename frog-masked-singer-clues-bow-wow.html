    Who is the Frog on The Masked Singer? All the revealing clues pointing to Bow Wow The US Sun

    14 Apr 2024, 00:00

    THE Frog performed for the second time on The Masked Singer on Wednesday, and fans are convinced Shad "Bow Wow" Moss is the celebrity behind the mask.

    Viewers have found many clues over the last two weeks and believe the rapper, 32, will be revealed as the Frog in the coming weeks.

    In the latest Masked Singer episode, viewers spotted a basketball lying around in the contestant's video clue package, which likely is a reference to Shad's 2002 movie Like Mike.

    They also saw the keys "C-S-I" highlighted on a typewriter, which might be signifying the rapper's role on the short-lived series CSI: Cyber.

    The undercover artist made his debut as part of Group B last week — in an episode revealing skateboarder Tony Hawk to be the Elephant — and that's when fans first realized the high probability of Bow Wow being the Frog.

    In that first clue package, Frog talks about his “metamorphosis” and how he left the spotlight like a “lightning bolt.”

    Fans on Twitter believe these are referring to the star going from Lil’ Bow Wow to simply Bow Wow and the lightning his character was hit with in Like Mike.

    That popular movie of his was believed to be referenced again when later on a microphone, aka mic, is shown with a thumbs up decal, aka “like” symbol, on it.

    Plus, a paper bag of hushpuppy “leftovers” is seen sitting on the ground, which viewers think is a shout out to the artist’s dog-themed stage name.

    There's also an advertisement for the 1996 Games, and the Olympics that year took place in Atlanta, which is where Bow Wow currently lives and also part of the title of the show he's on — Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta.

    To top it off, three bills —of $1, $5 and $100 denominations—are sitting on a brick ledge near the microphone, and they add up to $106, which is the number in the title of the BET show Bow Wow once hosted, 106 & Park.

    When given the chance to deny his association with the reality series, the actor instead nervously avoided The Real host Amanda Seales' question about him being the Frog when he visited the talk show on Wednesday.

    After laughing anxiously, he refused to give a definitive answer and chose to instead deflect.

    He simply said: “As far as I know...I’ve been on this show since probably last season [when] they thought the Rottweiler was me.

    “They had clues like ‘puppy love’ and all this little stuff, so I’ve been getting me being on this show for a long time, but I’m gonna be on tour so it’s kinda hard to juggle that and…”

    Co-host Loni Love jumped in and said: “Okay, so you not. You’re saying you’re not.”

    But he still never said those words or answered Amanda’s original yes or no question.

    He instead mentioned theory mistakes from last season and how he’s going to be busy with his upcoming tour, even though most of The Masked Singer Season 3 has already filmed.

    Though the Rotweiler turned out to American Idol alum Chris Daughtry last season, viewers are much more sure about Bow Wow’s role this time around because of all of the clues.

    Viewers will gain even more insight into the famous face hiding behind the Frog mask when a new episode of The Masked Singer airs Wednesday on Fox at 8 p.m.
