    Joseph McGinty Nichol, better known by his stage name McG, is an American director, producer and former record producer. He started his career in music, directing music videos and producing various albums. He then rose to fame with his first theatrical release, The Angels of Charlie, which had the highest weekend opening weekend for a first film at the time. Since then, he has directed a number of other films, including sequels to Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle, Terminator Salvation, This Means War, The Babysitter and The Babysitter: Killer Queen, co-creator of the TV series Fastlane. and produced many TV shows, like OC, Supernatural, and Chuck. McG also owns a production company, Wonderland Sound and Vision, founded in 2001, which has been overseeing the production of the movies and TV shows he's been working on since Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle.
