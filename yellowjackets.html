    Played by Kevin Alves

    Guarded, insecure, and sometimes obnoxious, teenage Travis struggles to be decent while using his mask of indifference to navigate his inexperience with girls and figure out how to live among them.

    KEVIN ALVES
    Kevin Alves is a Canadian born film and television actor most well-known for his most recent work on Showtime’s original series YELLOWJACKETS. He began his work in his teenage years with a debut role on a made for TV film called Kayaks Adventures of Gabe and Allie. His work in television during his teen years happened while juggling traveling the world to 20 different countries as a world class figure skater. Once reaching two World Figure Skating Championships and being ranked in the top 30 in the world, Kevin ended his skating career to focus solely on building his career as an actor. Within the next year,  Kevin worked on Syfy's Warehouse 13 and recurred role as Fabio on the 12th season of Teen Nick's Degrassi: The Next Generation. He then went on to recur for two seasons on both Freeform's Shadowhunters as Bat Belasquez from The Mortal Instruments and Netflix’s Locke & Key as Javi Gonzalez. Currently he can be seen as Travis Martinez in the first season (10 episodes) of Showtime’s genre bending new series Yellowjackets. The series has already been renewed for a second season and nominated for 7 Emmys and multiple Critics Choice and Writers Guild Awards.
