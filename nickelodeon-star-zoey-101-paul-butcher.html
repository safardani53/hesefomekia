    ZOEY 101 star Paul Butcher looks very different nearly two decades after the show - and he's got a very different job too.

    The actor, 29, was just 11 years old when he took on the role of protagonist Zoey Brook's (Jamie Lynn Spears) younger brother Dustin in 2005.

    Paul played the youngster for all four series between 2005 and 2008.

    The show, which won three awards, followed the life of a young girl called Zoey and her friends who attend a boarding school called Pacific Coast Academy.

    Fans were devastated when the programme came to an end 15 years ago - and there was speculation about it as it was revealed Jamie Lynn was pregnant at the age of 16 around the same time.

    However Jamie revealed in 2020 that the programme had wrapped up two months prior to the news being announced.

    Paul was joined on screen with Jamie Lynn Spears, Sean Flynn, Kristin Herrera, Christopher Massey, Alexa Nikolas, Erin Sanders, Matthew Underwood and Victoria Justice.

    The star shot to fame when he was just seven years old.

    He appeared in a number of TV shows and films before Zoey 101.

    Paul bagged himself roles in the likes of That '70s Show, NYPD Blue and Six Feet Under.

    Most read in TV

    After Zoey 101 Paul appeared in Avatar: The Last Airbender, American Dad!, Criminal Minds, and most recently, Destiny 7.

    Now Paul has been focusing on his singing career.

    His Instagram bio states: "Actor/ Singer - Owe everything to God."

    He's released a number of songs including Horses, Blood, Pillowtalk, Nothing and Freaky.

    Paul recently attended a festival and confessed that he dreams of when he will perform his tunes on stage.

    “I get this feelin.. I wanna be where you are..," he captioned the post.

    "Was truly inspired by this weekend at @hardfest. Can’t wait to be performing 'Pieces' at something like this one day.

    "Thank you to my friends - to the old ones and the new ones I made. You all inspire me.

    "The talent here inspired me. I’m feelin’ blessed."

    The star is also has a huge following on TikTok, boasting 1.1m followers.

    Back in January Zoey 101 fans were over the moon that it was revealed that there was going to be a reboot of the show.

    However Paul and former co-star Victoria Justice do not appear in it.
