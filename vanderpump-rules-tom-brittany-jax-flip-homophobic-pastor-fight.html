    Vanderpump Rules Brittany and Jax FLIP after Tom confronts them over homophobic wedding pas

    JAX Taylor and Brittany Cartwright LOST it in this weeks episode of the Bravo show when confronted about their homophobic wedding pastor by Tom Sandoval. The Vanderpump Rules couple announced they swapped their original officiant for NSYNCs Lance Bass, 40, after Lisa Vanderpump, 59, voiced her concerns.

    14 May 2024, 00:00

    JAX Taylor and Brittany Cartwright LOST it in this week’s episode of the Bravo show when confronted about their “homophobic” wedding pastor by Tom Sandoval.

    The Vanderpump Rules’ couple announced they swapped their original officiant for NSYNC’s Lance Bass, 40, after Lisa Vanderpump, 59, voiced her concerns.

    Brittany chose family friend Pastor Ryan Dotson despite the previous anti-gay quotes the church leader has been reported saying.

    In 2015, Bravo said he made the comment: ”The devil wants to legalize SIN. Just because it's legal that doesn't make it RIGHT!!!! How wicked is our nation becoming?”

    Ryan was also said to have made the statement: ”Let me first say, you can love people but not agree with this lifestyle or their choices.

    “With that being said, There are people that I care for that are gay, BUT I do not condone or accept this lifestyle as it is contrary to the Word of God.”

    The topic turned into a heated confrontation at co-star Peter Madrigal’s birthday party when Tom, 36, made the comment that “it took Lisa” to say something in order for the pastor to be axed.

    Brittany fired off at the Tom Tom bar owner: “You have the nerve to say it took Lisa Vanderpump to say something for us to do something about it? You do not know what I've been going through!

    “Seriously, you're like not even a real friend,” the upset reality star continued to scream.

    Her financed then shot back at his friend: ”Why are you f*****g trying to do something like this to somebody? Why are you trying to hurt somebody like this? You’re trying to make them look bad!”

    When Tom defended himself and claimed he was upset by the words Ryan preached, Brittany tearfully shouted: "That's why I fired him, and that's why I'm getting another pastor!

    "This is not my f--king fault! I did nothing wrong! We have done everything that we can do! I saw one comment, okay? One comment!"

    Tom fired back: "I could show you like five,” referring to the multiple comments that have been reported.

    “I saw none of that! I chose to believe him, okay? This was my friend. I'm so sorry that I didn't go back and look at comments from five f*****g years ago,” Brittany emotionally responded.

    The upset Bravo star continued: ”No one who is a real friend to me would do this! Two weeks away from my wedding. He's a best man. I did nothing wrong! So f**k you! F**k you, Sandoval!

    “You can f**k off. F**k off, Sandoval! He does not deserve to be in the wedding at all. I hope Jax knocks him out. Knock him the f**k out. I'm about to knock him the f**k out!”

    Jax furiously proclaimed he was “done” with his best friend: "There’s no coming back from that. He’s not in my wedding anymore. There's absolutely no way that guy's gonna be in my wedding party."
