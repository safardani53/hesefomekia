    JANA Kramer is an American country music artist and actress who began rising to fame in 2009 when she was on the show One Tree Hill.

    The singer has several chart-topping country songs and has continued her acting career, working on shows like Grey's Anatomy and more.

    Who is Jana Kramer?

    Jana Kramer is the voice behind the songs Why Ya Wanna and I Got the Boy.

    She also spent three seasons on One Tree Hill, where she was featured in 43 episodes as Alex Dupree, a troubled starlet hired by Brooke Davis (portrayed by Sophia Bush) to become the face of her clothing brand, Clothes Over Bros.

    Directors were so impressed with Kramer's performance that they moved the role of Alex Dupree from being a recurring character to being one of the main characters on the show.

    Kramer left One Tree Hill just short of its ending to pursue her music career, which included the self-titled 2012 album Jana Kramer and her 2015 album Thirty-One.

    She won the American Country Music Award in 2013 for Top New Female Artist.

    Is Jana Kramer in a relationship?

    While Kramer has been married a few times, and she has also dated some high-profile stars.

    On May 25, 2023, Kramer announced she was engaged. She made the announcement on her podcast, Whine Down. She is engaged to Scottish soccer coach Allan Russell.

    Just a month after she announced the engagement, she revealed she was pregnant with her third child. In June 2023, she confirmed that the baby will be a boy.

    Prior to her romance with Russell, she has been romantically linked to fellow country music singer Brantley Gilbert and former Chicago Bears quarterback Jay Cutler.

    She married Washington Commanders tight end Mike Caussin in 2015, and their marriage lasted until 2021.

    The pair share two children together, a daughter named Jolie Rae Caussin and a son named Jace Joseph Caussin.

    In April 2021, Kramer filed for divorce from Caussin, with court documents reporting the causes to be "inappropriate marital conduct, irreconcilable differences, and adultery."

    She was dating Navy veteran and fitness trainer Ian Schinelli following the divorce but later announced that the two broke up in April 2022.

    What is Jana Kramer's net worth?

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kramer reportedly has a net worth of around $2million.
