    Well, the 2018-19 NBA season is right around the corner, which means this is a particularly tense time for Khloe Kardashian.

    As you may recall, it was toward the end of his previous season with the Cleveland Cavs that Tristan Thompson was caught cheating on Khloe while she was pregnant with his child.

    Khloe is commonly regarded as the most strong-willed of the Kardashians, so fans were surprised when she failed to kick Tristan to the curb.

    Amazingly, almost six months after Tristan was caught being unfaithful to Khloe the two of them are still going strong as a couple.

    And the rest of the Kard clan is reportedly less than thrilled about it.

    "Khloe just can’t let this relationship go," one insider recently told In Touch Weekly.

    Members of Khloe’s inner circle reportedly believe that she and Tristan are riding it out solely for the sake of their infant daughter True.

    Now, there are rumors that Khloe and Tristan are seeking to strengthen their bond by welcoming a second child.

    And not surprisingly, Kim and company are less than thrilled by those reports.

    "All the Kardashians are against her having another baby, but she won’t listen to any of them," the source added.

    "She’s blinded by love when it comes to Tristan." 

    The insider goes on to say that the Kardashians are accepting of Khloe’s decision to give Thompson a second chance.

    But they are staunchly opposed to the idea of her doubling down on the relationship by either marrying Tristan or having a second child with him.

    "Tristan is making a fool out of Khloé, but she won’t see it," the source continued.

    "We all worry she’ll be pregnant and heartbroken once again." 

    One major fear among Khloe’s loved ones is that it’s not Tristan has no strong feelings for Khloe and is attempting to make the relationship work for the sake of his daughter. 

    "Tristan loves True," the insider explained.

    "He’s determined to play a big part in his daughter’s upbringing."

    Tristan has a child from a previous relationship, and he’s reportedly dismayed with how that relationship has worked out.

    Insiders claim the NBA star is consumed by guilt that he hasn’t been able to play as a big role in the life of his first kid as he had hoped.

    It’s admirable that Tristan is so committed to being a father to his two children … but hopefully he and Khloe both realize that guilt is no foundation for a solid marriage.
