    Kim Kardashian's actor ex Michael Copon welcomes first child, son Malachi, with wife Victoria Jade

    KIM Kardashians actor ex Michael Copon, who starred on One Tree Hill and Power Rangers, welcomed his first child with his wife Victoria Jade. The couple announced that they welcomed their son, Malachi Zamora Copon, over the weekend.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    KIM Kardashian’s actor ex Michael Copon, who starred on One Tree Hill and Power Rangers, welcomed his first child with his wife Victoria Jade.

    The couple announced that they welcomed their son, Malachi Zamora Copon, over the weekend.

    Michael, 38, shared the happy baby news with a collection of adorable photos on Tuesday.

    The actor posted close-up snaps of his sleeping baby boy, as well as ones featuring the whole family of Michael, wife Victori, and their son.

    In an emotional note, he wrote: “Sunday night 8:39 pm my life changed forever. My son Malachi was born! 

    “My wife was a warrior woman and delivered all natural not even a Tylenol. The respect I have for her is unexplainable… my son came out with the most beautiful natural instinct of looking for her nipple, the skin to skin contact brought tears of joy to my eyes.”

    The 38-year-old, who’s best known as  Felix Taggarro on One Tree Hill and the Blue Ranger on Power Rangers Time Force, continued: “We spent all day today bonding. I changed 5 poopie diapers and my wife fed him every two hours! 

    “Such an amazing day… I’m so in love with my child and I can’t wait to be the best dad in the world!

    The Beyond the Break alum previously dated Kim K and appeared in the premiere episode of Kourtney and Kim Take New York back in 2011.

    Michael's now married to Victoria and shares newborn son Malachi, while Kim is mom to four kids with ex Kanye West.

    Aside from OTH and Power Rangers, Michael’s also known for playing Boyz 'N Motion member Ricky on That’s So Raven, and Penn in Bring It On: In It to Win It opposite Ashley Benson and Cassandra Scerbo.

    Most recently, he played Colton Kurosaki on the drama series, The Bay, as well as Phil in the 2020 ION Christmas movie, Beaus of Holly.

    After welcoming his son into the world, Michael shared some more photos from a maternity shoot he did with his wife.

    Both of them posed topless, with the actor showing off his buff arms and chiseled abs, while Victoria showed off her growing baby bump, which her husband cradled. 

    He captioned the shots: “So we didn’t get to post these photos I took of me and my wife but since Malachi already came we figure better late then never! 

    “I still can’t believe my little guy was inside that belly only a few days ago! 

    “I love having our studio to be able to have the lights and backdrops all ready to go for any moment!”
