    Who Is Blake Griffins Girlfriend? Details onHis Dating History

    24 May 2024, 00:00

    Blake Griffin was famously dating Kendall Jenner back in 2017, and as with most things Jenner-Kardashian, the relationship made massive headlines. 

    However, the couple didn't last and broke up less than a year later. 

    Since then, Griffin has been fairly low-key with his love life, leading many to question: Who is Blake Griffin's girlfriend?

    These days, the Brooklyn baller is in a relationship with fashion designer Francesca Aiello. 

    Who is Blake Griffin's Girlfriend?

    Griffin's current girlfriend, Aiello, is a fashion designer. In fact, she was the youngest designer to be on Miami Swim Week when she debuted in the esteemed event back in 2014.

    She began her journey as a designer in 2012 after her mother encouraged her to have custom swimsuits made. 

    Blake Griffin's girlfriend fashion designer Francesca Aiello (Source: Instagram)

    Turns out, she had some real talent on that front. Her designs have been donned by supermodels and celebrities, including Gigi and Bella Hadid, Hailey Bieber, and Kylie and Kendall Jenner.

    "I was going to Hawaii to get them custom-made, and my mom suggested I get a few for my friends while I was there," the model and designer penned on her website.

    "I'd give all of my friends bikinis, then they'd go home and post them on Instagram and that’s when Frankies Bikinis started."

    Griffin and his designer girlfriend have been an item since 2018, shortly after he broke up with his former girlfriend Kendall. 

    Blake Griffin's Relationship with Kendall Jenner

    Kendall began dating Griffin sometime in September 2017, and the reality star could be seen courtside in many of her beau's games.

    Although she told Vogue (via Us Weekly) in March 2018 that she was happy with her relationship, news of Griffin's new girlfriend surfaced just months later. 

    Prior to that, the NBA star was engaged to fiancée Brynn Cameron. The duo called off their engagement in July 2017, as per a source with Us Weekly. 

    "They ended things on good terms and they are still friends while raising their son and daughter," the source revealed. 

    The former flames also share a son, Ford, and a daughter, Finley. 

    The news only broke out in September that year, and as per the outlet, the duo was still making public appearances together. 

    Around the same time, he was starting to make public appearances with Kendall. 

    Blake Griffin's Rumored Girlfriends

    Now, the time right after Griffin's breakup from Kendall is a tad bit blurry as the Brooklyn Nets player was making headlines with different women. 

    While some sources shipped him off with Aiello, others were still considering relationships with Madison Beer. 

    Another source with Us Weekly reported in July 2019 that Beer was with Griffin in his house in The Hamptons. 

    "Madison and Blake were also both at The Surf Lodge [in Montauk] together and hooked up," the source claimed. 

    By January 2020, yet another source with the same outlet swore that Griffin was dating Fox Sports Detroit reporter Brooke Fletcher following his 2018 split from Kendall.

    That is to be expected though, Griffin is, after all, known for keeping his love life to himself.
