    WITH Mother’s Day 2019 around the corner, this year you can celebrate three generations – and get your mum involved as well as your kids.

    Falling on March 31, all the ladies in your life can feel special with Matalan’s triple-matching pyjama sets.

    • Girls Peppa Pig Mothers Day Pyjamas, Matalan, £10 – buy now

    The department store is selling matching jammies for grannies this year, so your mum can in join the fun.

    With a set for mums featuring the slogan ‘world’s best mummy’, treat your own mother with another pair which says ‘number 1 grandma’.

    A kid’s pair is available for ages nine-months up to five years, which says ‘my mummy is the best’.

    And the best part? All the pyjamas are pink and feature Peppa Pig, a firm favourite in nearly every household.

    • Peppa Pig Mummy Pig pyjama set, Matalan, £15 – buy now
    • Peppa Pig Grandma Pig pyjama set, Matalan, £15 – buy now

    Ranging from £10 to £15, the website says: “If your little one can’t seem to get enough of Peppa Pig these cute pyjamas are a must!

    “The perfect attire for bedtime stories and watching Peppa Pig of course!”
