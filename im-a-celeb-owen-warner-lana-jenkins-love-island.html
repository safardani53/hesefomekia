    I'M A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here runner-up Owen Warner has broken his silence after his latest romance was revealed.

    The soap actor was revealed to have dated new Love Island hopeful Lana Jenkins in the latest episode of the ITV2 show.

    Owen, 23, who is best known for portraying Romeo Nightingale on Channel 4 soap Hollyoaks, headed out for a run as he ignored his former relationship reveal.

    The TV star ignored questions from fans after Lana, 25, admitted she had dated the soap hunk.

    Instead, Owen filmed himself following a sweaty run, as he posed for a video on his Instagram stories.

    Not addressing the romance, he said: "I feel absolutely mashed. Look how sweaty I am, gees!

    "Looks like I've been out in the rain and it's not even raining out there – I look like a shadow of myself. Why do I do this? What am I doing to myself?"

    It comes after Love Island star Lana revealed her very famous ex last night to new love interest Ron Hall.

    The group were playing a game of Never Have I Ever when some very interesting facts get revealed – including Lana having dated the soap hunk.

    She let slip that she used to date Hollyoaks and I'm A Celeb hunk Owen Warner as part of the game.

    Most read in TV

    She said: “My most recent ex-boyfriend is an actor”.

    Ron, 25, who is coupled up with Tanyel Revan but still interested in Lana, was surprised and impressed by the news.

    After the game Ron quizzed her and she explained to him: "It’s my ex-boyfriend… he’s called Owen Warner.”

    Ron joked back: “So basically I’m competing with someone who's just come runner-up on a massive show and who’s an actor and I work in finance, yeah, erm, sick. This is great.”
