    A MAJOR office supplies chain has announced it will be closing another location.

    Staples, a home office supply retail chain which has been in business since 1986, will be closing a location in Westfield, Massachussets.

    The Staples at 273B E. Main St. in the Westgate Plaza will be shuttering its doors on October 6.

    That means there's still time to grab necessary supplies for the school year before it shuts.

    It comes as the company announced they were closing other locations in the northeast.

    In April, a major Staten Island Staples store announced it was shutting its doors.

    The nearest one for Westfield customers is at 555 Hubbard Ave in Pittsfield.

    Long before the pandemic, Staples has been trimming its fleet, closing more than 200 stores in the process.
