    PARIS Hilton has opened up about her two baby children's unique names.

    The Hilton heiress has two children - a son, Phoenix, and a daughter, London - shared with her husband Carter Reum, 42.

    Paris, 42, is also the stepmother to Carter's older daughter Evie from his previous relationship with Laura Bellizzi.

    However, while some fans might think that her children, like Paris, were named after cities as a family tradition, the Simple Life star revealed that it's a tradition of her own, and she picked out her favorite locations as inspiration.

    She further elaborated on her I Am Paris podcast: "I've been planning my childrens' names for years and years.

    "I knew when I was a little girl I always wanted to have a daughter one day named London, because London is one of my favorite cities in the world.
