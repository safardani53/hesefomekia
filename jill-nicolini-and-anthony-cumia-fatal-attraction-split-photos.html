    Playboy model and television personality Jill Nicolini has reportedly split from Anthony Cumia, radio talk show host of The Opie & Anthony Show.

    Nicolini is a native of Long Island, New York and is a model, actress and traffic reporter for CW11 Morning News. She posed for Playboy under the name Jill Nikki in their Fall 2001 College Girls Special Edition. She ended up relinquishing her Miss Long Island 2002 crown, which kept her from running in the Miss New York pageant.

    In 2003, Nicolini appeared on the FOX reality show Married by America, where she reached the finals. On the show she used the name Jill Marie Nicoline.

    In May 2008 she began dating shock jock and fellow Long Islander Anthony Cumia. He has had issues with past infidelity and the relationship started to fall apart in August. Cumia shared with radio viewers that she is unstable and said that she went through his credit card statements looking for possible moments of infidelity.

    She apparently took his Escalade, EZ-Pass and laptop – holding them hostage. She reportedly dropped in to retrieve her belongings from Cumia’s home while he was entertaining another woman. World has it she threw some of his new girlfriends things into his burning firepit. Cumia reports that he had to pay Nicolini $1,000 in extortion money.

    Sounds like a reality show. We’re just saying….
