    Get your notepads ready, funnyman Deon Cole is dishing out advice.

    The comedian stopped by Us Weekly Video to play a round of the Celebrity Advice Game prior to the premiere of his first-ever stand up special, Deon Cole: Cole Blooded Seminar.

    Cole, who plays Charlie on ABC’s Black-ish, offered Sarah Silverman advice on how to not compete with boyfriend Michael Sheen’s ex-girlfriend Kate Beckinsale.

    “There’s nothing you can do,” Conan O’Brien’s former writer tells Silverman about competing with Sheen’s smokin’ hot ex Beckinsale, with whom he shares daughter Lily, 17. “Just got to accept it.”

    He also had some ideas on how Jay Z could handle wife Beyonce’s Lemonade, which — as fans are well aware — allegedly addresses his infidelity.
