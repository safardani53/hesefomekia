    If you’ve got it — well, you know what to do with it! Kylie Jenner snapped a mirror selfie in which she’s clad only in lingerie, and shared the pic via Instagram Thursday, September 15.

    The lip kit creator, 19, stripped down to just a blush-hued balconette bra and matching underwear for the racy photo. Also on display in the shot? Jenner’s stunning $6 million Hidden Hills mansion. As she’s standing a good distance from the oversize wall mirror she utilized for the shot, we get a small peek inside her newly renovated house, complete with casement windows, French doors and a mix of ivory and chocolate decor, including a furry lounge chair. 

    https://www.instagram.com/p/BKZOjkEheDX/

    Of course, the youngest member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan is hardly the first member of her famous family to post a saucy selfie — even that day! Almost simultaneously, her older sister Kim Kardashian shared a snap of her reflection — completely naked, save for one arm covering her breasts — via Snapchat. She can be heard saying, “I just did a midnight spray tan, you guys. Tanorexic.”

    The mom of two — North, 3, and Saint, 9 months, with husband Kanye West — shared a very similar picture in March, which caused a social media uproar at the time. Chloë Grace Moretz, Bette Midler and Piers Morgan all publicly bashed the star for the pic, with Moretz calling it “voyeuristic”: “I truly hope you realize how important setting goals are for young women, teaching them we have so much more to offer than just our bodies,” she tweeted at the time. (Their feud is still ongoing.)

    Nevertheless, Ariel Winter, Chrissy Teigen and Kardashian’s sisters, including Jenner, came to the reality star’s defense. “No one was body shaming @justinbieber when he posted his nude guitar photo so why @KimKardashian? #herbodyherchoice #InternationalWomensDay,” Winter tweeted. Jenner added, “Kim is a legend.”
