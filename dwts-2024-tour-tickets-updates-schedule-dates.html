    DANCING With The Stars is hitting the road this winter for a live tour.

    The tour is kicking off on January 11 in Richmond, Virginia, and will end on March 26 in Los Angeles, California.

    Julianne Hough is set to perform on select dates.

    Those performing in the 2024 tour are Alan Bersten, Britt Stewart, Brandon Armstrong, Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov, Rylee Arnold, Gleb Savchenko, and Emma Slater as the show's host.

    The VIP Package, Mirrorball, and Platinum presales will begin today at 10 am.

    For those who don't snag tickets this morning, the general sale is set for 10 am on Friday.

    Purchase tickets on Ticketmaster here and Stubhub here.
