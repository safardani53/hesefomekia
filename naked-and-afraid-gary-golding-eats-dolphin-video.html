    NAKED and Afraid star Gary Golding has been caught eating a dead baby dolphin that was washed up on the shore of a Beach in California.

    The TV star, who describes himself as the "Tarzan of Los Angeles" and "here for the children and animals of the world" decided to hack away at the sea animal to make sure it didn't go to waste - and even went on to describe it as "gammy" and "not seasoned the best."

    In the shocking three minute long footage published by TMZ, the bee specialist got his hands on the deceased baby dolphin which was lying on the shore of Laguna Beach, CA.

    After spotting the lifeless animal, he shouted: "I got a dolphin right here in the background the seagulls are savaging it, if I was in the wild, I relate to those seagulls I'd be doing the same thing."

    The wilderness survival expert began to cut into the animals bloody insides and justified his actions by saying: "I want to know if there's any plastic in the stomach. The seagulls are going to love me for this."

    He continued to hack away it and if the philanthropist couldn't do anything more shocking, he then stated to the camera that he was going to eat the dolphin's heart.

    Gary is most well-known for his stint on the reality show The Naked and Afraid, a bizarre reality programme which puts two strangers together to try and survive the wild.

    The participants literally have to strip back to bare basics - as they even have to partake in the programme naked.

    He continued: "So hey I'm the scavenger I'm making it afraid, so I'm going to live up to what I do. I'm going to eat dolphin heart and I guess I'll eat what you call dolphin back strap.

    "Now the thing is I don't want to let this thing go to waste. Something you see me do before on TV, now you can see me do right here on the coast of California."

    "Not letting this poor creature just die for nothing and go to waste I'm going to nourish myself with it."

    The gruesome video then cut to Gary cooking in front of a green portable BBQ which was covered corner to corner with the blood and guts of the dolphin.

    The survivalist wasn't impressed with the taste of the breathless fish as he described it's heart as "gammy" and "not seasoned the best."

    He ended the video with the line: "I've never done that before, I've never seen that done before."

    Gary has 2,641 followers on Instagram, 590 likes on Facebook and a YouTube account with over 65 videos of him doing crazy stunts.

    He first appeared on our screens on the Discovery Channel's Naked and Afraid and Naked and Afraid XL.

    On his official website he offers the chance for fans to purchase merchandise, book him for appearances and even get the nature lover to officiate their wedding.

    When Gary isn't eating baby dolphins, he can also be found teaching children about the planet, helping rescue bees, sharing his specialist expert survivalist tips and even gives guides to people among the Los Angeles wilderness.
