    A WOMAN has revealed that she has found the most incredible trousers ever - so if your wardrobe needs an upgrade, you’ll want to listen up.

    Fashion fan and M&S lover Lydia Rose explained that the high-street chain has released a stunning pair of trousers that are not only great quality, but are seriously flattering too.

    So if you struggle to find trousers that fit you properly, you’ll need to check this out - and then run to M&S. 

    The fashion influencer took to social media to model her new trousers, which she styled with a white halterneck crop top and a pair of trainers.

    The blonde beauty beamed with her buy and penned: “The ultimate trousers. M&S clothing is actually so good right now!”

    She then claimed: “Finally the trouser fit we needed.”

    As she modelled her new trousers, she said: “I wanted to order from M&S some cream trousers because I was like, there's nowhere that does cream trousers that aren't going to be see through.”

    Lydia then pointed to the buckle tab detail and beamed: “This bit is the best thing ever. It's a pull waist so that you can cinch the back.

    “So if you always struggle to get the perfect fit trousers, this is the best way to do it with a nice, good quality pair.” 

    If you fancy getting your hands on these crepe tab detail wide leg trousers, they are available to buy online and in store from M&S.

    The trousers are £39.50 and come in seven different colours.

    Sizes range from a size 6 to a size 24, and there’s three different length options available - short/petite, regular and long - but sizes are selling out fast, so you’ll need to be fast if you want a pair. 

    The trousers are super trendy and can be dressed down for a casual look, or paired with a nice top and blazer for a smarter style.

    The flattering wide leg and high waist design, paired with the belt loops, front darts and pressed creases create a smart, tailored look.

    Not only this, but there’s practical touches too - including side pockets and added stretch to the fabric.

    The easy-to-wear trousers are sure to be a wardrobe staple this season, combining classic and contemporary styles. 

    The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @fashioninflux, has clearly left many stunned, as it has quickly racked up 1,749 likes. 

    Social media users were stunned at the gorgeous trousers and many were eager to share their thoughts in the comments.
