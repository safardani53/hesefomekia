    ARIANA Grande covered up all of her arm tattoos for her intimate wedding to Dalton Gomez at the weekend.

    The 27-year-old's large butterfly and Pokemon inkings on her left arm were concealed when she wore her stunning strapless Vera Wang gown.

    Ariana also hid the Japanese symbols for "let's sing", which she has inked on her left forearm above a drawing of the Pokemon character Eevee.

    Fans noticed that she covered up an inking of Spirited Away character Chihiro on the inside of her right forearm.

    The singer got her first piece of body art in 2012 and has since added more than 50 tattoos to the collection.

    It's not known how exactly Ariana hid the large tattoos, although it's likely her glam squad - including celebrity makeup artist Ash K Holm - used body makeup to conceal them.

    Despite covering up her arm tattoos, the 7 Rings hitmaker chose to leave her hand inkings on show - inlcuding a large moon and sun on her left hand and several words written on the inside of her fingers.

    Her back tattoo was also exposed and could be seen in stunning photos Ariana shared of herself posing in the backless ivory gown.

    The inking is a quote from Jim Carrey's 1998 film The Truman Show and reads: "In case I don't see ya: Good afternoon, good evening, and good night!' which is one of the actor's most iconic lines from the film."

    The singer had the inscription written upside down on her left shoulder blade in June 2019.

    Ariana and Dalton tied the knot on May 15 in an "intimate" ceremony at her $6.75million Montecito, California home.

    On Wednesday, she posted a series of glamorous photos from the big day on Instagram.

    The couple, who began dating in January of 2020, said "I do" in a close-knit ceremony with just 20 guests.

    The Grammy-award winner was escorted down the aisle by both her mother, Joan Grande, and father, Ed Butera, which Vogue claimed was a "one of the most special moments for the bride."

    Dalton, 25, wore a Tom Ford suit as he waited to greet his girl at the alter underneath a flourish of beautiful white flowers hanging from the ceiling.

    The pair were engaged before Christmas of 2020, less than a year after meeting, but reportedly grew very close during the pandemic.

    The pop star announced her happy news with several photos of her stunning engagement ring alongside snaps of her former fiancée as well.

    Though Ariana and Dalton were first linked at the start of last year, she finally went public with her man by appearing Justin Bieber's Stuck With U music video in May 2020.

    A source recently spoke out about their relationship, saying that the 7 Rings singer was "so excited" to be engaged.

    "She has really enjoyed getting to know Dalton out of the spotlight," the insider explained.
