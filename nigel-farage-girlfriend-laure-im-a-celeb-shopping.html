    At one point, Laure managed to drop the bags as her hands were so full and she struggled to hold them all.

    Nigel is said to be the highest paid I’m A Celebrity contestant of all time – with a huge £1.5 million fee.

    Laure has previously not spoken out about life with Nigel, but shed some light on it when she landed in Australia.

    Speaking about their six years together, she revealed:  “Life at home with Nigel is very good.
