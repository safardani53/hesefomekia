    Anthony Mackie is a well-known American actor who made his playing debut in the semi-biographical drama film 8 Mile (2002). For his part in the LGBT drama Brother to Brother, he also received the Independent Spirit Award.

    Despite the fact that Mackie has been in a slew of blockbusters, it is his portrayal of Falcon, a superhero from Marvel Comics, that has catapulted him to stardom. He portrayed the role in multiple MCU films and most recently appeared in the TV program The Falcon and the Winter Soldier.

    With all of these accomplishments, he has finally decided to take a giant step ahead and construct a television and film studio. We’ll go over everything in greater detail in this post, as well as the actor’s net worth.

    Anthony Mackie is rumored to be constructing a film and television studio

    What is the location of the studio?

    Anthony Mackie purchased a 20-acre tract of land on Interstate 10 Service Road near reading Boulevard to develop a television and film studio. He’s always wanted to be a producer, and it appears like his dream is about to come true.

    The new studio will aid Louisiana in the establishment and expansion of a film and television industry, which will provide prospects for land purchase and development.

    What Do The Politicians Have To Say About It?

    The current Mayor of New Orleans, LaToya Cantrell, was ecstatic with the concept and couldn’t stop herself from expressing it on Twitter. LaToya Cantrell isn’t the only well-known figure who is enthusiastic about this idea. Oliver Thomas, a member of the City Council, is also enthusiastic about the proposal, saying,

    “We used to joke about becoming Hollywood South.” “I believe we’re now in a position to make it happen, and New Orleans East can serve as the basis for that,”… “I believe that having Captain America and his brand, as well as his star power, helps to sustain all of the other investments.”

    Anthony Mackie’s Net Worth

    When someone is so dedicated to establishing his manufacturing facility, he is likely to have a significant amount of cash on hand. Let’s take a look at Anthony Mackie’s net worth as we wrap up this post.

    Mackie has an estimated net worth of $8 million. His monthly salary exceeds $200,000, and he charges a good amount for the films and television series in which he appears.
