    Michael Giacchino is an American musician and composer for film, television and video games. He received an Oscar, a Primetime Emmy and three Grammy Awards. He is known for his collaboration with J. J. Abrams, Brad Bird, Matt Reeves, Pete Docter, Colin Trevorrow, Jon Watts, Drew Goddard, The Wachowskis and Thomas Bezucha. His film scores include several Mission films: Impossible, Jurassic Park, Marvel Cinematic Universe and the Star Trek reboot series, seven films from Pixar animation studios such as The Incredibles and The Incredibles 2, Super 8, Dawn of The Planet of the Apes, Zootopia, Rogue One: A Star Wars Story and the upcoming Spider-Man: No Way Home and The Batman movies. He is also the composer of the video game series Medal of Honor and Call of Duty and the television series Alias, Lost and Fringe.
