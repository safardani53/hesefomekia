    Doug Aldrich is a Los Angeles-based hard rock guitarist. He formed the group Burning Rain with Keith St. John in 1998 and previously performed with the groups Whitesnake, Dio, Lion, Hurricane, House of Lords, Bad Moon Rising and Revolution Saints. He is currently part of The Dead Daisy group. He has also released several solo albums. In 2015, Doug toured as the guitarist of former bass singer Deep Purple and singer Glenn Hughes. In early 2016, it was announced that he would be replacing Richard Fortus as guitarist for The Dead Daisy.
