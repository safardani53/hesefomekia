    Allen Neal Jones is an American professional wrestler. He is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the Raw brand under the ring name AJ Styles and is currently one half of the Raw Tag Team Champions in his first reign. Styles is also known for his time in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling from 2002 to 2014 and New Japan Pro-Wrestling from 2014 to 2016. Styles debuted in 1998 and competed for various independent promotions before gaining initial mainstream exposure in World Championship Wrestling in 2001. Styles became widely known after signing with TNA, who described him as "the cornerstone of the company since [its] inception"; he won the TNA World Heavyweight Championship twice, the NWA World Heavyweight Championship three times, and was the inaugural titleholder of the X Division Championship, which he won six times. He is also the first TNA Triple Crown and the Grand Slam champions.
