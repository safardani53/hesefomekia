    PARIS Fury spent a whopping £500 in B&M on Christmas decorations and presents for her huge brood. 

    Earlier this week, the mum-of-seven was also spotted picking up matching Christmas outfits for the Fury family in Primark. 

    The 33-year-old said that this is set to be her busiest Christmas yet after welcoming newborn son, little Rico, into the family in September. 

    Paris is famed for being down-to-earth and not letting her and husband Tyson’s combined £51 million net worth stop her from shopping on the high-street or bargain hunting.

    “With Christmas just around the corner, I’m getting prepped and ready early for an even busier Christmas this year with our new arrival,” she said.

    “Christmas tree, decs and pressies all sorted with B&M Stores - and the selection and choice is incredible.

    “I’ve managed to get everything I need and also some fabulous bargains on toys with the 2 For £20 offer. 

    “The range is incredible. I was absolutely spoiled for choice and found something for all the kids in there.”

    Taking to Instagram, Paris shared a video of her mega shopping haul. 

    Among the bargains she picked up giant ‘Champagne’ baubles for £18, luxury baubles for £5, candy cane tree decorations for £3 and ‘We Believe Sweet’ baubles also for £3.

    To give them a home, Paris picked up B&M’s 7ft Pre-Lit Cambridge Christmas Tree for £130. 

    She also nabbed a wreath reduced to £30 from £20, Christmas stockings for the fireplace for £4 and ‘woodland’ red velvet bows for £5. 

    By way of toys, the busy mum picked up a Numskull Gaming Locker for £20, Playstation goodies, two Barbie dolls for £20 as part of a deal and some mega Lego sets. 

    Everything she purchased came to approximately £500.

    “I love how you have millions on the bank yet do this yourself and shop on the high-street,” one fan commented on Paris’ video. “Stay humble always.”

    “I love the fact you’re a well off family, yet you still shop in bargain high-street shops like the rest of us,” another echoed. 

    Earlier this week, Paris unveiled everything she’s purchased from Primark ahead of Christmas.

    She said that visiting Primark is one of her favourite things to do during the yuletide season, tipping that the Fury clan will be rocking matching pyjamas this year.
