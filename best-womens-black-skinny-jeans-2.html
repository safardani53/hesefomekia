    How do I wear black jeans?

    They're a staple for a reason.

    Black skinnies can be paired with pretty much anything and can be worn on any occasion.

    Pop on a basic white tee and a black leather jacket for a trendy monochrome look, or go for a controversial double denim and add a blue denim jacket.

    From heels and boots to sandals or trainers, you can combine a flattering pair of black skinny jeans with any shoe and still look stylish.

    Can you wear skinny jeans if you have big thighs?

    If you have larger or athletic thighs, you know it can be a challenge to shop for jeans that won’t emphasise their size.

    But don’t despair, as plenty of models are cut to flatter larger legs.

    Usually, fashion experts say that the best style for women with bigger thighs is the straight leg jean, as they will make you look taller.

    There are also flare jeans, which are slimmer on the hips area and wider in the bottom - a winner for those with muscular thighs.

    But if skinny jeans are what you have set your eyes on, there's no reason to give up.

    First of all opt for darker shades, which will have a slimming effect and will elongate your legs.

    And wear your skinny jeans with a long and flowy top, which will camouflage the problem area.

    Finally, put some wedges or platform-style heels to create balance in the line of your leg and create a stronger look.

    Can you wear black skinny jeans to a wedding?

    Black skinny jeans might not be the first option that comes to mind when you're deciding on what to wear to a wedding, but they can work. Hear us out.

    Your best black skinnies are a great staple to wear with a colourful blouse, heels and lots of accessories – the right styling can look super smart.

    Just make sure that whatever you wear with the jeans is a brighter colour to make sure that you don't look like you're going to a funeral.

    Think pastels and nude tones teamed with fashion jewellery and a statement clutch.
