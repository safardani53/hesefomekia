    MEN'S grooming has come a long way since the days when a good scrub with a bar of soap sufficed.

    The best body washes for men now pack a punch with natural ingredients, long-lasting fragrance, and high street to high-end options.

    But with so many products available, it can be hard to choose what's best for you.

    Sensitive, dry skin? Pick out a gel that contains natural skin-calming goodies like aloe vera, and avoid any synthetic ingredients and fragrances.

    Whether you're looking for a multitasking all-in-one hair and body wash, a product that's luxurious and indulgent, or an affordable everyday option, we've got you lathered.

    We've tracked down the best body wash for men below, so you'll want to lather, rinse, repeat more than ever.

    1. Best body wash for men: Star buy

    • Sukin for Men Body Wash 500ml, £7.46 from Mankind - buy here

    Looking for a product that ticks all the body wash boxes? We've found it.

    This Sukin Body Wash cleanses and hydrates with a mix of organic and natural ingredients, like skin-softening aloe vera, baobab extract, and jojoba oil.

    Cinnamon and citrus scents uplift and energise for an invigorating kickstart to the day, and it's all wrapped up in a cool apothecary-style pump-action bottle.

    Did we mention it's also 500ml so you get a lot of bang for your buck?

    Shower time, sorted.

    2. Best cheap shower gel for men

    • Bulldog Skincare For Men Original Shower Gel 200ml, £3.49 from Feel Unique - buy here

    High-quality doesn't have to mean high-price.

    Bulldog's brilliant shower gel is packed with seven essential oils, moisturising aloe vera, and deep-cleaning menthol to give your body the perfect pep-up prep for the day ahead.

    It's also vegan and free from artificial colours, synthetic fragrances and parabens. 

    3. Best smelling men's body wash

    • Molton Brown Black Peppercorn Body Wash 300ml, £22 from Look Fantastic - buy here

    Want to really turn up the nose-pleasing smell-good factor?

    This Molton Brown body wash is a sensation for the skin and your senses.

    Strong and spicy, the Madagascan black peppercorn oil mixes with basil oil and coriander oil for a super-fresh cleanse and lingering fragrance.

    It looks good on the bathroom shelf, too.

    4. Best men's body wash for dry, sensitive skin

    • Shea Moisture African Black Soap Soothing Body Wash 384ml, £11.99 from Boots - buy here

    This soothing body wash is packed with skin-calming natural ingredients.

    The gentle formula gets rid of grime with skin-soothing ingredients like oat extract, while moisturising aloe has a calming effect on dry, sensitive skin.

    Vitamin E also fights against external aggressors to keep your skin cleansed and calm.

    5. Best luxury men's body and hair wash

    • Clinique Happy For Men Body And Hair Wash 200ml, £24.50 from Debenhams - buy here

    Turn up the heat in the shower with a luxury treat-yourself body wash.

    This foaming wash leaves you with a citrus-fresh fragrance while getting rid of dirt and impurities from head-to-toe... literally, as it also doubles up as a hair wash.

    Also, the bright orange packaging will give a zing to any bathroom cabinet.

    6. Best multitasking body wash for men

    • American Crew 3-In-1 450ml, £14.50 from Mankind - buy here

    Sometimes, you just want an easy life. And nothing could be simpler than this shampoo, conditioner and body wash in one.

    A host of natural ingredients work to soften skin, invigorate the senses, stimulate your scalp and detangle hair.

    It's a multi-tasking, time-saving marvel that smells pretty good, too.

    The Nuxe Men's Multi-Use Shower Gel is also another fab option for time-poor fellas who love to look and feel good.
