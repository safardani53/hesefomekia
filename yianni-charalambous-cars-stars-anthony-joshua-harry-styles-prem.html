    But who is he and what is his net worth? Here's everything you need to know.

    Who is Yianni Charalambous?

    According to his two Instagram accounts which boast millions of fans and followers, Yianni Charalambous is a celebrity Supercar customiser and social media star.

    He is also the owner of Yiannimize, a star-studded garage based in north London.

    From incredible vinyl wraps, to complete interior overhauls – Yianni delivers the goods when it comes to the most extravagant car customising requests.

    His work over the years has seen him wrap and design stunning interiors on rare and expensive motors including Lamborghinis, Ferraris, Porsches, and Bentleys for a number of big names in the worlds of sport and entertainment.

    His A-list clientele includes the likes of Harry Styles, John Terry, and Gordon Ramsay.

    But Yianni has one person to thank for his rise to the very top – former Arsenal player Bacary Sagna.

    Speaking to The Sun in 2019, he said: "My first footballer was Bacary, who is my best friend to this day. He’s painted on the wall in the garage because he helped me out.

    "I customised his Range Rover, wrapping it in gloss white. I also fixed an amazing sound system and put some TVs in there too.

    "He asked me to deliver it to the training ground and the reaction he got from his teammates was incredible, they all thought his car looked awesome."

    And his work with sports stars doesn’t stop there.

    In June 2023, Jack Grealish used some of his well-earned cash to splash out on customising a Lamborghini Performante Urus.

    The sports car, which is light blue like his club’s home strip, is said to be worth between £205k and £210k before personalisation.

    But Grealish paid extra to get his new motor wrapped by celebrity car customiser Yianni Charalambous.

    The new car was shown off by Charalambous on his Instagram story - and it came complete with a joke about City beating his team, Arsenal, to the title.

    During the clip, which was posted to his story, he is walking next to the car and says: “So there it is guys, it is completed, Lamborghini Performante, tiffany blue.

    “This car belongs to Jack Grealish, the Man City Premier League winner. Yes, he took it away from the Arsenal, however he wanted his car wrapped.

    “I think it works perfectly, especially the way he plays for Man City, and there it is in all its glory. An incredible looking car.

    “He's gone shuts and returns, you've got the carbon everywhere that we've left exposed, nice choice of colour. It's going to look great on the training ground.”

    Utilising on his car-focused career, the ambitious self-made entrepreneur landed himself a television series in 2018 titled ‘Yianni: Supercar Customiser’, on UKTV’s entertainment channel, Dave.

    The series has also been broadcast on BBC Three.

    Aside from achieving social media success on Instagram, Yianni boasts over 2 million subscribers on YouTube and over 90,000 followers on X - formerly known as Twitter.

    What is Yianni Charalambous’ net worth?

    According to The 24/7 Project, the business owner and social media sensation has an estimated net worth of $2.5 million.

    This converts to £2,050,500.00.

    What TV shows has Yianni Charalambous appeared in?

    Since 2018, Yianni has had his own TV series called Yianni: Supercar Customiser for three seasons.

    His series is once again being shown on the small screen on BBC Three on January 9, 2024.

    The first episode will show Yianni do his magic on reality television star Sophie Kasaei from Geordie Shore's Velar car.
