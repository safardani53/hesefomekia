    KATIE Waissel's prostitute nan is STILL working in the sex industry at the ripe old age of 87.

    Sheila Vogel-Coupe was revealed to be the UK's oldest sex worker during her granddaughter's disastrous stint on the X Factor back in 2010 - and six years later, she's still on the game.

    A website specialising in "mature escorts" reveals the genteel OAP is currently working under a pseudonym in a well to-do area of London.

    Here, the pensioner's love of sex is laid bare as she informs would-be clients of what they can expect from a romp between the sheets with her.

    Her most recent review describes Sheila as a "lovely lady who clearly knows exactly what to do and which of my buttons to press."

    Other satisfied customers describe her as "charming and sensual" and "very talented".

    One glowing review reads: "I was greeted by [Sheila] wearing lingerie and red lipstick.

    "She was welcoming and friendly, and talkative, and we chatted for about 15 minutes before going off to the bedroom, where [she] definitely took care of me, she was very sensual, and a very good and willing kisser.

    "She seemed genuine in enjoying our time together, as did I!"

    Another enthuses: "The twinkle in her eyes and her lovely smile makes you feel truly welcomed."

    The Sun online attempted to contact Sheila, but was told that she "Would not talk to anyone!" by an employee of her escort agency.

    The pensioner's love of sex shocked Britain - and Katie - when her secret life of vice was revealed in November 2010.

    However, despite Katie and her family disowning her, defiant Sheila said she was not ashamed of being a £250-an-hour prostitute or of her appearance in a porn film.

    In a statement, Katie’s sister Natalie Davis said: “We were completely shocked and devastated to hear the news that my grandmother has been conducting immoral activities, including prostitution and pornography.

    “We, as a family, disassociate ourselves wholly from these activities and from any future stories that arise from them.”

    Sheila said: “I work as a prostitute because I love it. It gives me great satisfaction and keeps me young.

    “I could go on for years yet. Why should I stop?

    “I think of myself as a refined lady giving a very special service men are not going to get anywhere else.

    “I’ve had a few celebrity clients but I would never say who they were. They worked in the music industry and TV and things.

    “I like to make men happy and content. I have no inhibitions. I love people. I’m an outgoing personality who lives in the 21st century.”

    Sheila turned to escorting after the death of her husband in 2004, finding solace in the company of her punters - some of whom were in their early twenties.

    She caused more heartache for her family when she appeared on This Morning to blast Katie for costing her business.

    Speaking to Eamonn Holmes in January 2011, she explained that her granddaughter's exposure on The X Factor had left her too exposed and scared off multiple clients.

    She said:  “Absolutely she [Katie] has ruined my career. I’m afraid she has. But I still wish her well. I love them all.

    “I love her and it breaks my heart to see my family split up like that. I would just ask them to not be so judgemental.”

    However, her only regret, she admitted, was starring in hardcore porn movie Freddie’s Great British Granny Bang, an experience she told the presenters she just wished she could forget.
