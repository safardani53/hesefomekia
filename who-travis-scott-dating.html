    Who is Travis Scott dating?

    Regarding dating, Travis Scott is arguably best known for his relationship with Kylie Jenner.

    After hitting it off at Coachella, the pair began dating in April 2017 and have been on and off ever since.

    On Saturday, January 7, 2023, Us Weekly announced the couple split after spending the holidays in separate places.

    "Kylie and Travis are off again, they were supposed to spend the holidays together, but she went to Aspen to be with her family and friends up there," a source told the outlet.

    "This has happened so many times before; they're known to be on again off again but always remain friends and great coparents."
