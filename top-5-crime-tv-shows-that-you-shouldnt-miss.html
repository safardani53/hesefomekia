    Top 5 Crime TV Shows That You Shouldnt Miss

    Crime TV series have fans and following all around the globe. Nothing beats staying at home and binge-watching ones favorite criminal TV drama. Crime TV series are audience favorites, whether its a fantastic murder mystery or a string of unresolved cases.

    02 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Crime TV series have fans and following all around the globe. Nothing beats staying at home and binge-watching one’s favorite criminal TV drama. Crime TV series are audience favorites, whether it’s a fantastic murder mystery or a string of unresolved cases.

    With a rising interest in crime dramas, production companies have developed a number of great crime series in recent years. These programs, which surely appeal to fans from all over the globe, are full of suspense, mystery, chase, and excitement. This list of the five finest crime TV programs will be a delight for all crime series fans seeking some gripping crime shows since they are intriguingly thoughtful and extremely binge-worthy.

    Top 5 Crime TV Shows You Shouldn’t Miss

    We have picked the finest criminal TV series just for you.

    1) Sinner

    The Sinner is one of the most highly regarded criminal TV dramas of all time, with dramatic, dark, philosophical, and emotional material. The anthology series is skillfully weaved to provide viewers with a mentally interesting experience.

    The anthology series, created by Derek Simonds, revolves around Officer Harry Ambrose, played by Bill Pullman, a police detective who solves severe and bizarre criminal cases, becoming emotionally involved with each case. It is rather astonishing how all four seasons of the series have covered the complete path of those who have perpetrated the crime, as well as Detective Ambrose’s dogged hunt for the truth. The Sinner’s first three seasons, as well as its most recent season 4, which premiered in 2021, are presently available on Netflix.

    2) Mare of Easttown

    Mare of Easttown is a limited series featuring Oscar winner Kate Winslet in an emotionally charged criminal thriller. Brad Ingelsby is the series creator and writer, while Craig Zobel is the limited series director. The series follows police investigator Marianne “Mare” Sheehan, played by Winslet, as she works tirelessly to investigate the tragic death of a young mother while also fighting to regain control of her own life, which is falling apart.

    Watch Mare of Easttown, which is presently available on Disney+. The limited series has received widespread recognition and has been nominated for the 16 73rd Primetime Emmy Awards. Kate Winslet received an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Lead Actress.

    3) Only Murders in the Building

    Only Murders in the Building is a crime-mystery series with a comic twist that viewers will enjoy binge-watching. This 10-episode crime-comedy series, created by John Hoffman and Steve Martin, has received a lot of praise for its unique and humorous approach to crime stories, enthralling direction, and incredible acting from the lead cast members, including Steve Martin as Charles-Haden Savage, Martin Short as Oliver Putnam, and Selena Gomez as Mabel Mora.

    The plot centers on three strangers who are obsessed with a certain real-life criminal podcast. Season 1 of the show premiered on the popular streaming site Hulu on August 31, 2021. The series has been renewed for a second season, which will premiere exclusively on Hulu on June 28, 2022. Watch Season 1 of the crime-comedy series, which is presently available on Hulu ahead of Season 2’s debut.

    4) Ozarks

    Ozark, the Byrde family epic, is without a doubt one of the finest criminal drama series of all time, with its ambition for power, unexpected demise, and riveting stories. It has earned enormous fame and acclaim from both reviewers and audiences all around the globe throughout the years.

    The Netflix series, created by Mark Williams and Bill Dubuque, is a criminal TV program fan’s dream, with compelling performances from the principal actors, jaw-dropping twists, and fascinating stories. The series stars Jason Bateman as Martin “Marty” Byrde, Laura Linney as Wendy Byrde, Sofia Hublitz as Charlotte Byrde, Skylar Gaertner as Jonah Byrde, Julia Garner as Ruth Langmore, and a number of other talented actors. The story, which revolves on the engrossing journey of the Byrde family into the world of money laundering and narcotics, has become a fan favorite throughout the years.

    5) Lupin 

    Since its premiere in 2021, the French crime-mystery thriller Lupin has been Netflix’s most-watched non-English series. It is, without question, one of the most engrossing crime-mystery programs of all time. The series, created by François Uzan and George Kay, is based on Maurice Leblanc’s novel Arsène Lupin. The series tells the tale of Assane Diop, a great thief played by French actor Omar Sy.

    Ludivine Sagnier as Claire, Ludmilla Makowski as the young Claire, Clotilde Hesme as Juliette Pellegrini, Léa Bonneau as the young Juliette Pellegrini, Nicole Garcia as Anne Pellegrini, and numerous more round out the series’ main cast. Lupin Part 3 has been officially renewed by Netflix. Lupin Parts 1 and 2 are presently available exclusively on Netflix.
