    CHERYL looks moody for her latest advert which sees her pouting and strutting in knee-high boots - just a day after being branded a diva.

    The pop star, 35, shared a video on Instagram of herself plugging hair extensions dressed in tiny shorts and leather crop top.

    In the short clip she can be seen strutting her stuff for the camera, whilst flicking her long hair for affect.

    The new advert for Easilocks hair extensions comes as Cheryl was accused of diva behaviour at her first gig in four years on Sunday.

    She played at Hits Radio Live in Manchester and insisted all rooms and corridors were cleared when she needed to use them.

    My backstage source told The Sun: “Cheryl did a great job on her Instagram profile of making it look like she was the most accessible A-lister ever, posting pictures with Mabel and showing videos of her goofing about dancing in her dressing room. But the reality could not have been more different.

    “Cheryl had everyone clear her dressing room and the corridors and surrounding areas whenever she was near. She only posed with Mabel, who is a singer like herself, for a photo while that was happening.

    “She did very little other Press either, except for the photocall and quick chat with Hits Radio’s Sarah-Jane Crawford.”

    The behaviour came at the end of a tough week which saw her X Factor comeback performance panned for being awkward and generated 100 Ofcom complaints from furious viewers who reckoned it was too raunchy for Saturday night TV.

    The routine included her licking her hand in an ill-advised move from choreographers, and ended with her collapsed in a heap on the stage floor.

    That led to a a worried Simon Cowell asking in a whisper if she was OK.

    Cheryl hoped performing on her old X Factor stomping ground would help secure chart success.

    But her comeback has failed to impact, with the single entering at a disappointing No19 before limping down to No45 in one week.
