    WHEN times are tight, people are looking for any way possible to save money.

    And with groceries one of the biggest expenses for the majority of families, the chance to fill up the freezer for a tenner is one to jump on.

    So it makes sense that savvy shoppers are racing to Farmfoods, after a new deal was introduced where you can get an incredible 10 Birds Eye items for just £10.

    One person took to the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group on Facebook to share the incredible deal, writing: "Any 10 for £10.00 farmfoods".

    They then added: "Update for people asking how long the offer is on for.

    "There is no expiry date set for this as yet and will be ongoing for the time being."

    Considering you get 10 chicken fingers in one box, that means you can effectively get 100 chicken fingers for just £10.
