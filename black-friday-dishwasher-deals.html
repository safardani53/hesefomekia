    When is Black Friday 2021?

    Black Friday takes place on November 26 this year. That's the day after our friends across the pond celebrate US Thanksgiving.

    But the Black Friday sales often begin a couple of weeks before that, with retailers offering great deals earlier in November as well, and the discounts continuing through the weekend.

    You can keep up with the latest news, deals and updates on our Black Friday topic page.

    More Black Friday Appliance Deals

    When is Cyber Monday 2021?

    Cyber Monday takes place on November 29. It's an online-only extension of those Black Friday deals, with retailers often adding extra sales and discounts for web purchasers.

    This is also your last chance to get a good deal, so don't miss out.

    Is Black Friday a good time to buy a dishwasher?

    Absolutely! If you're planning to make a major purchase like a dishwasher, then the discounts that come this November with Black Friday can make a big difference to how much money you're shelling out.

    You will also see your favourite brands getting competitive over who can offer the best deals, so keep an eye out: you'll be able to find a quality product at a great price.

    How much do dishwashers cost?

    Depending on the size and brand, you could pay as little as £150 to £200 for a basic slimline dishwasher.

    However, top of the range full size dishwashers can cost over £1,000.

    On average, you'd expect to pay between £250 and £600.

    How long do dishwashers last?

    A good dishwasher should last you around 10 years.
