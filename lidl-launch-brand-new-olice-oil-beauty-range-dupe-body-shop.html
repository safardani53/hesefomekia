    Lidl is launching a brand new olive oil beauty range from 1.99 &amp; its a dupe of The Body Shop

    LIDL has launched a brand new olive oil beauty range with every item less than 2 - and its a dupe of The Body Shops pricier version. The supermarket is launching its range tomorrow, called The Pure Olive Collection, with everything just 1.99.

    26 Apr 2024, 00:00

    LIDL has launched a brand new olive oil beauty range with every item less than £2 - and it’s a dupe of The Body Shop’s pricier version. 

    The supermarket is launching its range tomorrow, called The Pure Olive Collection, with everything just £1.99.

    There’s Face Cream, Body Lotion, Shower Gel and Hand Cream to choose from, all made with organic olive oil.

    Lidl's collection is remarkably similar to The Body Shop’s Olive range, but a tub of nourishing body butter will set you back £16.

    Some olive oil shower gel from the high-street store costs £5.50, while a tube of body lotion is £8.50

    Announcing their range, Lidl said: “All products within the range are made with organic olive oil which boasts a number of beauty benefits due to its hydrating nature and high antioxidant content.

    “The intensive care moisturising formulas leave skin feeling super soft and are perfect for all skin types.”

    They added their range has a “subtle floral scent” and leaves skin feeling moisturised for 24 hours. 

    All the products, which are vegan, will be available to buy in-store tomorrow.
